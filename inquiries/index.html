INQUIRIES
The Norman Gershman Photographic Archive was established in the summer of 2021 and is housed in Montpelier, Vermont; The Archive contains all of the works of Norman Gershman. While still in its formative stages as the Archive is organized, printing of newly rediscovered work is already underway. ​PRE-ORDERS will soon be available and each month's more prints will be added to the available list as they are properly evaluated and prepared. ​Should you be interested in any particular image you see in the Archives, please do let us know. We look forward to hearing from you. Eric Gershman, Trustee and Director eric@normangershman.com Martine Bisagni, Archivist and Curator mia@normangershman.com
